Meranda

Meranda is still in early development, with vertical slice expected by the end of 2014.

Meranda is a platform RPG game that is easy to learn and play as expected in platforms, yet the gameplay and story is progressive. The player must complete all the levels in an open open world, collecting items and abilities, all while managing health and mana bars.

Story
Play as Kaylee or River, two little girls that have been turned to snails by a mischievous wizard. Kaylee and River must traverse the world and collect colored crystal shards to defeat the wizard to return to their original forms.

World
The story takes place in Meranda, a world in another dimension. Meranda is divided into six territories, each ruled by a magical crystal shard.

Whats Unique?
This game stands out in the market with a heroine main characters, color based battle system, and mixing platform and RPG adventure game mechanics.

Goals
The game will be designed in three phases.
The first phase being the vertical slice and alpha (tentative) with all/most game mechanics developed to show as a personal portfolio submission, designed and developed by Brandan Haertel
The second phase being a team process utilizing Agile processes to create alpha (tentative), beta, release, and updates
The third phase is to update the game to have a new feature (multiplayer) that should effect all the code of the game, giving the developers experience revisiting and refactoring code to include new features from a stake holder.
