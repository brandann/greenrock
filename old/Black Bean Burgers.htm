Black Bean Burgers

 

·         1          lb         Dried Black Beans

·         1          Cup     Water

·         ¼         tsp       Cumin

 

1.      Boil Black Beans on high for 1 hour

2.      Combine drained and rinsed black beans with water and cumin

a.      Smash into a paste

3.      Form into 4oz patties

4.      Fry until heated through, and browned.

5.      Enjoy!
